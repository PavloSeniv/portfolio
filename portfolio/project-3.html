Project Overview

The "Alivio" project is a wellness platform designed to help users manage and overcome stress through personalized journaling. It offers a guided experience where users complete a survey to personalize their journal, then engage in writing and reflection exercises tailored to their specific stressors.

The platform addresses various emotions, such as anxiety, anger, or sadness, and provides tools to help users become aware of and manage their stress. The website also features success stories to demonstrate its effectiveness.
